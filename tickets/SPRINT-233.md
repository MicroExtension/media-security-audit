# Sprint 233 - Wizard Field Trial Readiness

## Goal

Prepare the guided audit wizard for real pilot usage by showing a field trial
checklist before audit creation.

## Scope

- Add a final review checklist for live-check readiness.
- Cover authorization, scope, tools, outputs, stop path, and customer window.
- Keep the checklist informational.
- Do not change scanner execution.
- Do not change mission authorization or scope approval rules.

## Acceptance Criteria

- The review step shows field trial readiness checks before creation.
- The checklist is visible in the template and responsive on narrow screens.
- Tests verify the template and CSS hooks.

## Safety

- No scan execution added.
- No network activity added.
- No authorization bypass added.
